Data Science Track Requirements

Upper Level Concentration

Complete 12 credits at the 300-400 level in an area outside of CMSC. Chosen in consultation with your CMSC advisor.

***No course cross-listed with CMSC may be used for the ULC.

- The Upper Level Concentration area does not auto populate. Your assigned advisor will need to manually move your ULC-eligible courses to this area during degree audit checks.

  • NEEDS: 12.0 CREDITS, 1.700 GPA

Lower Level Requirements

  • SELECT FROM: MATH 140, MATH 141
Computer Science Courses
  • NEEDS: 6 COURSES

Statistics and Advanced Math
  • NEEDS: 2 COURSES
